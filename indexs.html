Automatic Highs & Lows Ultimate indicator.

Make your trading easier with automatic drawings and alerts.

Features.

Automatic highs and lows.

  • Automatic higher highs, higher lows, lower highs, lower lows.

  • Automatic lines connecting the highs and lows.

  • Change point colours automatically according to the trend.

  • Customize colours for individual drawings.

    •

Automatic ChoCh and BOS.

  • Automatic change of character for uptrend and downtrend.

  • Automatic Break Of Structure with dashed lines.

  • Customize colours for individual drawings.

    •

Automatic Order Blocks.

  • Automatic Order Blocks with customizable colours.

  • Adjust the size of the Order Block according to your needs.

  • Display options: ‘latest order blocks’ or ‘major order blocks’ or display ‘all order blocks’ (selected ‘major order blocks’ in the given image).

    •

Multi Time frame indicator.

  • Apply the indicator in higher time frame while you are in lower time frame.

  • Import the indicator multiple times and apply them on different time frame.

  • Customize colours for individual drawings.

  • Individually customize each indicator.

    •

Automatic alerts.

  • Automatic alerts for BOS, ChoCh and Order Blocks.

  • Receive alerts in all your devices.

  • Set your own notification sound for alerts.
